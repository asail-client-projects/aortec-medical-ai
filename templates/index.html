About AORTEC

At Aortec, we transform vascular care with cutting-edge 3D modeling technology. Our platform creates patient-specific, printable aortic models that not only replicate anatomy with remarkable accuracy but also predict growth dynamics and rupture risk using advanced algorithms. By combining high-resolution imaging, biomechanics, and machine learning, we empower clinicians to make informed decisions, tailor treatment strategies, and improve outcomes in aortic disease management. Aortec is where innovation meets clinical insight, shaping the future of aortic care.
